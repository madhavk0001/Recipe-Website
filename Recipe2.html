Maggi Noodles – 2 Minute Student Recipe

This is one of the most popular and easiest recipes among students. It takes very little time and always tastes delicious.

Ingredients Required

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Maggi tastemaker
  • Butter (optional)
  • Chopped vegetables (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles.
  3. Add the tastemaker.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add butter if you want better taste.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 3 minutes

Difficulty Level: Very Easy

Best For: Quick Meal / Night Study Snack
