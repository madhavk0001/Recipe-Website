Red Sauce Pasta – Quick and Tasty Recipe

This pasta recipe is simple, tasty, and perfect for beginners. It is a great option for students who want a filling meal.

Ingredients Required

  • 1 cup pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Chilli flakes
  • Butter
  • Water

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil water in a pan.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 6–7 minutes.
  3. Drain the water.
  4. Add butter in a pan.
  5. Add tomato sauce and mix.
  6. Add the boiled pasta.
  7. Sprinkle salt and chilli flakes.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes and serve hot.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Best For: Lunch / Evening Meal
