Veg Sandwich – Quick & Healthy Breakfast Recipe

This veg sandwich is one of the easiest and healthiest recipes that anyone can prepare in just a few minutes. It is perfect for students and beginners who want something tasty without spending too much time cooking.

Ingredients Required

  • 4 bread slices
  • Butter
  • Cucumber slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Green chutney (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Take two bread slices and apply butter on one side.
  2. Add cucumber, tomato, and onion slices.
  3. Sprinkle a little salt and black pepper.
  4. Cover it with another bread slice.
  5. Cut the sandwich into triangles.
  6. Serve fresh and enjoy.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Best For: Breakfast / Evening Snacks

Healthy or Not? Healthy

Tips for Better Taste

  • Always use fresh vegetables.
  • You can toast the bread if you want it crispy.
  • Add cheese if you want more taste.
