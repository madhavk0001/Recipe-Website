Banana Milkshake – Healthy and Quick Drink

This milkshake is very healthy and easy to make. It is perfect for breakfast and gives quick energy.

Ingredients Required

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 glass milk
  • 2 spoons sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Honey (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Cut the banana into small pieces.
  2. Add milk into the blender.
  3. Add banana pieces and sugar.
  4. Add ice cubes.
  5. Blend for 20 seconds.
  6. Pour into a glass and serve fresh.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Best For: Breakfast / Healthy Drink
