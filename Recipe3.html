Chocolate Milkshake – Easy Summer Drink

This is a simple and refreshing milkshake made with very few ingredients. Perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients Required

  • 1 glass milk
  • 2 spoons chocolate syrup
  • 2 spoons sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Chocolate powder (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Add milk into a blender.
  2. Add chocolate syrup and sugar.
  3. Add ice cubes.
  4. Blend for 20–30 seconds.
  5. Pour into a glass and serve cold.

Recipe Details

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Best For: Summer / Dessert Drink
